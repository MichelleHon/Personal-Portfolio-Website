TGD Media Representation Research

Conducted qualitative analysis on 4 interviews and online discussions to identify 3 key themes in the TGD communities’ perspectives on media representation.
Revealed persistent frustration in the TGD community with stereotypical media portrayals and identified a strong call for more authentic, complex, and normalized representation for TGD characters in mainstream media.
